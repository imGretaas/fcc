Who I am

Hi, I'm Francesco Pistore and I'm a student of the MSc Finance in Venice, Italy. Of course this has nothing to do with my online jobs.. well! Here I have some space to explain what happened in the last years that brought me here. If you have questions feel free to send me a message via the contact form! :)

I am interested in many things:

  • Photography and Travel
  • SEO
  • Web
  • Finance

My passion of photography started at age 14, in 2006, when I received my first pocket camera. I started to make some photos but I noticed, some years later, that I was doing more vlogs than photos and that was strange during first 2000s.
Some years later I stole my mum's D90 and I made her go around Europe to shoot amazing pictures.

During 2009, when I was 17, I founded my first website that was called “pistonet”. Some years later, in 2012, I discovered WordPress with its plugin, themes and, most of all, the SEO.
Since that discovery a world has opened up to me and I started with another big and great adventure: “Stintup”.
Stintup is a magazine and my job during my university years. Thanks to Stintup I traveled a lot, I visited more than 25 countries, and I achieved press membership to important events like MWC in Barcelona, Geneva International Motor Show, AME Padua and EXPO 2015 in Milan.

Last but not least, I attended the University of Ca' Foscari in Venice, Italy. I did my Bachelor's degree in Economics and Management and, after that, I took a MSc in Finance.
I have a big passion for financial markets since 2013, year when I started training and investing. Some years later I discovered the magical world of FinTech, where my two passions merge together; IT and Finance.
During 2017 I started to study cryptocurrencies and the technology behind it (blockchain), some months later I started investing on it.
